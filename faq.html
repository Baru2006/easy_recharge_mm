Frequently Asked Questions

How to order a service?

Go to the "Order Form" page. Select the category and service you need, specify the quantity, choose your payment method, and provide the transaction ID from your payment. Finally, click "Submit Order".

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are processed within 5 to 15 minutes. During peak hours, it might take up to 30 minutes. You can check the live status of your order on the "Status Live" page.

What payment methods are supported?

We currently accept payments via Wave, KBZPay, AYA Pay, and CB Pay. More options may be added in the future.

How do I perform a P2P exchange?

Navigate to the "Exchange Money" page. Select the payment method you are sending from and the one you want to receive to. Enter the amount you sent, and the system will calculate the fee and the amount you'll receive. Enter your transaction ID and submit the request.

How can I contact support?

For any issues or inquiries, please contact our support team via email at support@easyrecharge.mm. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
