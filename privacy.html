Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 17, 2025

EasyRecharge MM ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you.

Data Storage and Security

All transaction data submitted through our forms (Order Form and P2P Exchange) is stored securely in a private Google Sheet. Access to this Google Sheet is restricted to authorized administrative personnel only. We implement reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

Public Status Page

To provide transparency, a public status page is maintained. This page displays transaction information sourced from a JSON file hosted on GitHub. This JSON file is generated from the data in our Google Sheet. To protect your privacy, we only display a truncated version of your User ID (e.g., the last 4 digits) and never display your full phone information or transaction ID on the public page.

Admin Notifications

For operational purposes, when a new order or exchange request is submitted, a notification is sent to our administrative team. This notification is delivered via a private Telegram channel and an email to our admin account. This allows us to process your request promptly.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking services on our website. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Our operations rely solely on Google Workspace (Sheets, Apps Script), GitHub (for the public data file), and Telegram (for admin notifications).

Your Consent

By using our site, you consent to our website's privacy policy.
